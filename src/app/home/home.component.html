KookXtra

Passion of our local cooks ensures that you are never far from the traditional delicacies of an authentic home kitchen.

If you are a food lover and you want a break from the routine of cooking your meals but still want to be served a home cooked meal or you are craving for a traditional cuisine, you have found the right service because at KookXtra you get to taste the authentic recipes of our home cooks prepared with utmost quality, delivered right to your dining table.

Your meals are cooked by your neighbours who are passionate about cooking and want to share traditional dishes that they learned from their mum or when they travelled.

Since it is traditional and home cooked, you will find these delicacies are not something that can be cooked in rush. Plan your meal and enjoy the local traditional dishes at the comfort of your home.

All our food are prepared with good quality ingredients, with no preservative or tinned food.

You will also be able to view the nutrition label for every dish along with the full list of ingredients. You know exactly what goes into your meal.

And we also have dashboard for you to track your nutrition intake against the recommended nutrition levels.

We provide freshly cooked meals everyday delivered to your doorstep. We want to ease the process of planning your meal by providing you option to plan for 7 days, lunch and dinner which we will be delivering for you daily at a set time.

What more, you can enjoy multi-cuisine from multiple kitchen in one transaction. So, no compromise for anyone and you can have the meal together (and you pay delivery fee only once)!

Our promise to Home Cooks

We strongly value great quality and high standards of hygiene during food preparation.

All registered home cooks undergo a full onboarding process where we train them about the platform and its usage but most importantly we use the onboarding process to educate them on maintaining top rated food quality and hygiene. We at all times ensure that our Home Cooks have:

01
A proper kitchen setup
All our home cooks receive a visit from the KookXtra officer to help them set up a proper, safe and workable kitchen. We also provide a start-up kit to our home cooks which has all the necessary items for proper cooking
02
Knowledge of Food hygiene
During the Cook on-boarding process KookXtra Hygiene and safety officer train the home cooks on HACCP guidelines around food hygiene and quality parameters. This ensures that the home cooks prepare food with utmost quality.
03
First time Food Tasting session
Undergo food tasting session during which our friendly food evangelist and bloggers try the food and give their feedback. This helps our home cooks to make any needed changes to their recipe to appeal to our consumers.
